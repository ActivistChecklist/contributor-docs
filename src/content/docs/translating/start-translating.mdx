## Create an account on Crowdin
We use [Crowdin](https://crowdin.com/project/activistchecklist) to coordinate that review process. Anyone can contribute.

<Steps>
1. **Read the [code of conduct](/getting-started/code-of-conduct/)** so you know what we expect when you collaborate on translations.

1. **Create a free Crowdin account.** You can use an anonymous email address and username if you prefer (e.g. [Tuta](https://tuta.com)). If you want help deciding whether that matters for you, read [Protecting your privacy](/getting-started/protecting-your-privacy/).

    <LinkButton
      href="https://accounts.crowdin.com/register?continue=https%3A%2F%2Fcrowdin.com%2Fproject%2Factivistchecklist"
      icon="external"
    >
      Create a Crowdin account
    </LinkButton>

1. **Join our Crowdin project.** You can review suggestions, edit strings, and vote on or approve translations.

    <LinkButton href="https://crowdin.com/project/activistchecklist" icon="external">
      Join the Activist Checklist project on Crowdin
    </LinkButton>

1. **Tell us your username:** Fill out the [contact form](https://activistchecklist.org/contact/) here so we can promote you to a "proofreader."
    * Include your **Crowdin username** so we can promote you to a "proofreader."
    * Include your **Signal username** so we can be in touch moving forward.
</Steps>

## Start translating

<Steps>
1. **Read the [translation style guide](/translating/translation-style/)** to understand how to translate the content in a way that matches our voice and tone.

1. **Find a file to work on.** Go to [our Crowdin project](https://crowdin.com/project/activistchecklist) > Select a language > Look for any files that don't say 100% > To the right of the file, click on the three dots button **⋮** > **Proofread**.

1. **Edit or approve strings:** Find strings that do not have a green checkmark ✅ next to them. Approve items that look good. Edit items that need imrpovement.
</Steps>

If you have questions, refer to the [translation style guide](/translating/translation-style/) or contact us through the [contact form](https://activistchecklist.org/contact/).
